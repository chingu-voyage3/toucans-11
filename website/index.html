What We Do

We are a non-governmental non-profit organization that operates in Argentina, based in the Province of Córdoba, with the mission of promoting a harmonious relationship between man and nature.

To achieve our mission, we developed three action programs.

CONSERVATION PROGRAM: This program aims to conserve biodiversity at its five levels of organization: genetics, species, communities or ecosystems, regional or landscape and cultural.

EDUCATION AND COMMUNICATION PROGRAM: This program aims to transform biodiversity conservation strategies into actions.

MANAGEMENT PROGRAM: This program has, among other objectives, to collaborate with as many people, physical or legal, organisms and institutions, public or private, national or foreign, to act in those fields and have the same or similar objectives.
